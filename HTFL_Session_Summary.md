## Options Exercise Strategy (Future Decision)

**Context:** In ~3 years (~2029) you plan to leave HeartFlow. At that point you'll have ~50,598 vested options to exercise within 90 days.

### The Critical Constraint
ISOs must be exercised within **90 days of departure** or they lose their tax-advantaged status and the full spread becomes ordinary income (~45% tax vs. recoverable AMT).

### Recommended Strategy: Phased Exercise While Employed (Scenario C)
Exercise ~8,300 ISO shares per year in 2026–2028 while still earning $462K. Each batch creates ~$228K of AMT preference, but your salary ensures regular tax >> tentative minimum tax, so each batch is recovered immediately. At departure: no AMT bill, no 90-day cash crunch.

### Alternative: Exercise at Departure, Retire, Sell ISO Shares (Scenario B2)
**Your insight that selling ISO shares for diversification IS the AMT recovery mechanism is correct.**

When you exercise ISOs at $30 and then sell at $30 as a qualifying disposition (hold 1+ year):
- Regular LTCG: $27.78/share → you pay LTCG tax
- AMT adjustment: -$27.78/share → AMTI drops to near zero → tentative min tax ≈ $0
- Regular LTCG tax >> $0 → AMT credit absorbed

**This negative adjustment ($833K total) is permanently fixed** regardless of where the stock goes after exercise.

Recovery timeline (retirement, $30 stock):
- Year 2031: Sell $2.22 lot → recover $49K AMT
- Year 2032: Sell $2.72 lot → recover $97K AMT
- Year 2033–2034: Sell $8.33 ISO lot → recover remainder
- **Full $228K recovered in 3–4 years without a new job**

### 90-Day Risks (if exercising at departure)
1. **Miss the window** → ISOs convert to NSOs → $149K extra permanent tax
2. **Grant expiry timing** → 1,712-share $8.33 NSO expires **Sep 20, 2029** → leave before Jul 22, 2029 or pre-exercise this grant
3. **Cash not ready** → need $405K liquid within 90 days → pre-position in cash before departure
4. **Sell too soon** → must hold 12+ months → need 1 year of living expenses in separate cash
5. **Stock price risk** → at $60/share, combined exercise cost + AMT bill ≈ $900K cash needed
